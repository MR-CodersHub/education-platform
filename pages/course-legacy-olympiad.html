Program Overview

The Legacy Olympiad Program is our most prestigious offering, designed exclusively for top 1% mathematics students preparing for international competitions like IMO, USAMO, and elite university admissions.

This intensive 20-week program combines rigorous problem-solving training, advanced mathematical theory, and personalized mentorship from former Olympiad medalists and world-class mathematicians.

What You'll Master

Advanced number theory and combinatorics
Olympiad-level geometry and inequalities
Functional equations and polynomials
Graph theory and discrete mathematics
Competition strategy and time management
Proof writing and mathematical communication

Program Curriculum

Phase 1: Foundations (Week 1-5)

5 Weeks

Advanced number theory, combinatorics fundamentals, and problem-solving techniques

Phase 2: Advanced Topics (Week 6-12)

7 Weeks

Olympiad geometry, functional equations, inequalities, and graph theory

Phase 3: Competition Preparation (Week 13-18)

6 Weeks

Mock competitions, past Olympiad problems, and intensive practice sessions

Phase 4: Final Sprint (Week 19-20)

2 Weeks

Strategy refinement, mental preparation, and final assessments
