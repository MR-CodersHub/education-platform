Course Overview

Dive deep into the world of advanced algebra with this intensive 8-week program designed for students ready to tackle complex mathematical challenges. This course bridges theoretical algebra with practical applications in computer science, artificial intelligence, and competitive mathematics.

You'll master polynomial equations, matrix operations, abstract algebra concepts, and develop the logical reasoning skills essential for success in STEM fields and competitive exams.

Matrix algebra and linear transformations
Abstract algebra fundamentals (groups, rings)

Competition-level problem solving
